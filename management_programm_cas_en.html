Integrated Management and Strategy

In this module you will learn how to strategically align companies in complex markets, shape transformation, and effectively implement integrated management. Central concepts covered include:

  • Strategic transformation based on the St. Gallen Management House
  • Managing change processes in dynamic market environments
  • Application of the St.Gallen Integrated Framework to secure future success
  • Innovation and efficiency as elements of integrated corporate management

Financial Management

This module strengthens your financial management competence and teaches key instruments for well-founded decisions and sustainable growth. Topics include:

  • Analysis and interpretation of balance sheets, income statements, and cash flows
  • Decision-making based on financial and operational key figures
  • Strategic liquidity planning, investment calculation, and risk assessment
  • Use of modern tools and AI-based forecasting methods

Inspirational Leadership

In this module you will further develop your leadership personality and learn to lead clearly, empathetically, and future-oriented in challenging situations. Focus areas include:

  • Self-leadership, reflection, and evolution of your leadership style
  • Conflict resolution, communication, and decisive action in team contexts
  • Avoiding typical leadership mistakes and building psychological safety
  • New leadership requirements in digital and AI-influenced work environments

Marketing, Sales and Brand Communication

This module shows how marketing, sales, and brand management work holistically within the framework of integrated management – strategically sound and technologically supported. Topics covered include:

  • Customer understanding through market analytics, psychology, and AI-supported insights
  • Development of integrated brand and sales strategies
  • Implementation of effective communication along the customer journey
  • Positioning and differentiation in a dynamic competitive environment

Coaching & Peer-Learning

Transfer what you've learned directly into your own leadership practice – through individual guidance, practical reflection, and concrete implementation planning. Key elements covered include:

  • Executive coaching for leaders and leadership candidates for personal development
  • Application of content to your own leadership, strategy, or decision-making situations
  • Exchange in a protected environment to expand your own perspective
  • Structured reflection and feedback formats to support practical application
