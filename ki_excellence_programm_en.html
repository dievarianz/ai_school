Integrated Management and Strategy

In this module, you will learn how to develop a holistic management approach and successfully implement business strategies. Key concepts covered include:

  • Strategic positioning and competitive advantages
  • Integrated management in the VUCA world
  • Development of sustainable business models
  • Strategic analysis and decision-making

Financial Management

This module provides you with a comprehensive understanding of financial concepts and their application in the corporate context. Topics include:

  • Financial analysis and key figure interpretation
  • Investment assessment and decisions
  • Cash flow management and corporate financing
  • Financial risk assessment and hedging

Inspirational Leadership

In this module, you will develop your leadership skills and learn how to inspire and motivate teams. Key topics include:

  • Modern leadership styles and their effectiveness
  • Emotional intelligence and its importance for leaders
  • Change management and transformation leadership
  • Team building and conflict management

Marketing, Sales and Brand Communication

This module focuses on modern marketing strategies and effective brand management. You will deal with:

  • Strategic brand positioning and development
  • Integrated marketing communication
  • Digital marketing and omnichannel strategies
  • Sales psychology and customer journey optimization
