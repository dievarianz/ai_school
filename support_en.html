Frequently Asked Questions

Here you'll find quick answers to the most frequently asked questions.

What requirements do I need to meet to participate?

For participation in our Executive Seminars, at least 3 years of professional experience and a leadership position or aspiring leadership role are typically required. Additional requirements may apply for specific programs. Detailed information can be found on the respective program page or by contacting our admissions team.

How does the application process work?

The application process consists of the following steps:

  1. Online application via our website with CV and letter of motivation
  2. Review of your documents by our admissions committee
  3. If pre-selected: Invitation to a personal or virtual interview
  4. Admission decision and sending of registration documents
  5. Confirmation of your participation by paying the registration fee

The entire process usually takes 2-3 weeks.

Are the courses offered in-person or online?

We offer various formats depending on the program:

  • In-person formats: Intensive learning experience on-site with direct exchange and networking
  • Hybrid formats: Combination of in-person and online phases for maximum flexibility
  • Online formats: Fully digital programs with interactive live sessions

Detailed information about the format can be found on the respective program page.

How are the programs structured in terms of timing?

Our programs are designed to be compatible with professional commitments and offer various time structures:

  • Executive Seminars: Compact 2-5 day intensive seminars
  • Certificate Programs: Modular programs over 3-6 months with monthly modules
  • Executive Education Programs: Longer programs over 9-12 months with flexible learning phases

The exact dates are announced well in advance so you can plan your professional commitments accordingly.

What does a typical course day look like?

A typical course day at StGallen imt Business School is structured as follows:

  • 08:30 - 09:00: Check-in and networking coffee
  • 09:00 - 12:30: Morning session with interactive lectures and discussions
  • 12:30 - 13:30: Lunch together with networking opportunity
  • 13:30 - 17:00: Afternoon session with practical workshops and case studies
  • From 17:00: Optional: Networking events or time for group work

For online formats, the daily schedule is adjusted to avoid digital fatigue, with more breaks and shorter learning units.

What learning materials are provided?

Participants in our programs receive comprehensive learning materials:

  • Digital Learning Platform: Access to our Learning Management System with course materials, recordings, and interactive exercises
  • Course Materials: Scripts, case studies, and presentation slides in digital form and as print versions on request
  • Literature: Selected books and articles depending on the program
  • Access to Digital Library: Comprehensive scientific resources for in-depth learning
  • AI Tools: In our xAI programs, you get access to special AI applications for practical exercises

All materials remain available for at least 12 months after the course ends to sustainably deepen what you've learned.

What payment options are available?

We offer the following payment options:

  • One-time payment (with early bird discount)
  • Installment payment (in 2-4 installments depending on the program)
  • Invoicing to your company

All payments can be made by bank transfer or credit card. For questions about individual payment arrangements, please contact our finance team.

Are there funding opportunities or scholarships?

Yes, we offer various funding opportunities:

  • Scholarships for Executives: Performance-based partial scholarships for outstanding candidates
  • Diversity Scholarships: To promote diversity in leadership positions
  • Corporate Discounts: When registering multiple participants from one company

In many cases, our programs can also be tax-deductible. For personal advice on funding opportunities, please contact our advisory team.

What degrees and certificates will I receive?

Depending on the program, you will receive the following qualifications:

  • Certificate of Attendance: For Executive Seminars with confirmation of the covered content
  • Executive Certificate: After successful completion of a certificate program with credit points
  • Executive Diploma: After completion of more comprehensive programs with examination requirements

All certificates are issued by the imtxAI Business School in collaboration with the University of St. Gallen and are internationally recognized.

Are the degrees internationally recognized?

Yes, all our degrees and certificates are internationally recognized. The imtxAI Business School works closely with the University of St. Gallen, which is among the most renowned business universities worldwide. Our programs meet international quality standards and are regularly accredited.

In many cases, the acquired competencies can also be credited as credit points for further academic programs. Details on creditability can be obtained from our academic advisory team.
