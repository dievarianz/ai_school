Integrated Management and Strategy

Discover the seven key modules of our Executive Seminar – from management fundamentals to successful implementation of strategies in your company.

Concepts & Strategic Relevance of AI

Understanding AI – beyond the hype. Grounded knowledge for strategic decisions.

A structured overview of central AI concepts and concepts is presented. The relevance for your own company and industry is highlighted.

Strategic Application Fields and Business Impact of AI

Where AI creates real value – data-based, scalable, relevant

Concrete application areas of AI for value creation, efficiency enhancement, and customer loyalty are analyzed. Best-practice examples from various industries are discussed.

Data Strategy as Enabler for AI

AI only works with data – the right strategy makes the difference.

The development of an effective data strategy as a foundation for AI initiatives is taught. Participants learn which data are relevant and how they can be made accessible.

Business Model Innovation through AI

AI changes not just processes, but entire business models.

Participants develop how AI can transform existing business models and create new ones. Methodologies for systematic innovation are applied.

Leadership & Organizational Development in the AI Era

AI changes leadership, collaboration, and organizational structures.

Necessary leadership competencies and organizational changes for successful AI integration are taught. The importance of change management is emphasized.

Roadmap to AI Transformation

The structured path to successful AI integration – from vision to implementation.

A systematic approach for the AI transformation process is developed. Methods for prioritizing initiatives, building competencies, and cultural integration are taught.

Scaling & Governance of AI Initiatives

Pilot projects are just the beginning – now it's time to scale.

Approaches to scaling and institutional anchoring of AI are presented. Structures for governance, ethical guidelines, and success metrics are discussed.
